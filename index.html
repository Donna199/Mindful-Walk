Section 05 — Our Solution

SAFE WALKING
FOCUS MODE
FOR IPHONE.

A new iPhone Focus Mode that reduces distractions while walking, limits access to only the apps you choose, and protects your attention at crossings.

01
📱

Choose What Stays Open

During setup, users choose which apps are available in Safe Walking mode and which people or apps can send notifications.

Custom allowed app list
Priority contacts and apps only
Other alerts saved in Notification Center
02
🚶

Turns On Automatically

After setup, the phone uses GPS to detect when the user is walking outside and automatically activates Safe Walking mode.

GPS-based outdoor walking detection
Starts without extra taps
Keeps essential tools available
03
🚦

Protects Crossings

When the user is near an intersection or crossing the road, background apps like music pause until the crossing is complete.

Detects intersections and crosswalks
Pauses background audio and media
Resumes after the road is crossed
About Us

DESIGNED BY
PURDUE UX
STUDENTS.

We are UX Design students at Purdue University exploring how thoughtful design can improve pedestrian safety. Our goal is to bring attention to the everyday reality of distracted walking and create a solution that helps people stay aware, present, and safer while moving through the world.
